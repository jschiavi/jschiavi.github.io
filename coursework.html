Theory and Practice of Multimedia Production (CAP 3020)

Lines of Conquest

Created using Unity, GarageBand, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop. Spring 2016.

  • 2D side-scrolling Role-Playing Game (RPG).
  • Throughout gameplay you recruit new allies, upgrade soldiers and weapons.
  • Three types of gameplay: lane battles, non-combat cutscenes, and boss battles.
  • Retro, 8-bit aesthetic. Pixel art assets and low-sampled audio.
  • Final game delivered with complete merchandise packaging, game manual, and promotional material along with a full demo day.

Audio Lead

  • Composed, recorded and mastered all songs used during gameplay. 19 songs in total.

Other Contributions

  • Story: plot development and themes for the narrative, character personalities and appearances, level design, town profiles.
  • Art: concept art, sprites, packaging and promotional material.
  • Tech: boss battle mechanics, game engine animations (controllers, scripts, and prefabs), Scene setup, boss battle script, full audio integration.
